🧠 About Me 🧠

A Professional Research Scientist in Robotics and AI with a passion for research, innovation, and sharing knowledge. With 10+ years of experience developing machine learning, robotics, and data science solutions to address real‑world challenges, I hold a Ph.D. in robotics and information technology from Deakin University and have served as an Alfred Deakin Postdoctoral Research Fellow, with a focus on autonomous systems, medical robotics, and health data analytics. I have led and contributed to high‑impact, interdisciplinary projects funded by NASA, the National Science Foundation (NSF), and the U.S. Department of Transportation, as well as collaborations with the Australian Department of Defense, Australian Bureau of Statistics, and Austin Health. I demonstrate a strong track record of technical leadership, cross‑functional collaboration, and innovative solutions across different domains and sectors. This website serves as a digital profile to showcase my interests and contributions.
